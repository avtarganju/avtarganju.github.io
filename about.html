About Avtar Ganju


Avtar Ganju has a PhD in Pharma, an executive management degree in marketing and is a Certified Financial Planner (C.F.P), Chartered Life Underwriter (CLU) and a Chartered Financial Consultant (Ch.FC).

Avtar has been in the financial industry for over 35 years. He used to host a very popular TV show “Money Matters” on Indian Television in early 90's. Avtar Ganju was a member of the Top 50 Club at MONY, the life insurance company. Avtar held the sales manager positions both at MONY and MetLife. He was a Managing Director at MONY and MetLife. He has trained 100's of life agents.

Currently, Avtar specializes in Retirement Planning and Wealth Transfer with a focus on Legacy & Charitable Planning. Avtar has 600 plus endorsements on his LinkedIn Profile.

Outside of his career, Avtar is committed to various community organizations. He is the founding member of US Chapter of Salam Bombay Children’s Foundation - one of the most respected Charitable Organizations in India. He is the Treasurer and a member of the Board Of Directors. Avtar is an adviser to OPTIMIST - an Organization devoted for the betterment of poor children in Bangladesh.
